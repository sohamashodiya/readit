Welcome to Readit!

A simple reading log with a bunch of extra features.
Made for avid readers by a casual reader. (seriously)

Stages

All books added to Readit will go through these phases.
Planning to Read
  • Book details can be edited.
  • Tags can be edited through the edit modal.
Currently Reading
  • Book details are read-only.
  • Tags can be edited through the tag manager.
Finished Reading
  • Book details are read-only.
  • Tags can be edited through the tag manager.
All changes will be added to a timeline.

Tags

Organize books with your own categories.
No specific format required. You maintain the system.
Get tag suggestions using the ISBN of the book.
Add and remove tags from multiple books in one go.

Migrating Data

Keep your progress with you!
Easily download all your data as one file.
Backups can be instantly uploaded to resume progress.
